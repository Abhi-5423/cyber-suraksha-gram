Golden-hour warning

Report financial fraud immediately via 1930 and the National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal.

The national cybercrime portal and helpline are official Government of India reporting channels. Users should report financial fraud as quickly as possible because early reporting improves the chance of freezing fraudulent transactions.

Open Official Portal
  1. Call 1930 immediately for financial cyber fraud.
  2. Keep UTR number, bank name, transaction time, amount and screenshots ready.
  3. File complaint at cybercrime.gov.in and note acknowledgement number.
  4. Call your bank to block cards, net banking and suspicious transactions.
